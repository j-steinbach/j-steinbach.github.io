I've been asking myself if it is better to suppress all 'future' dreams to not get swayed or to really accept and live them. Basically 'oh, we are totally traveling together next year, I'll meet his/her parents, this is how it will go.'

I think that those hopes stem from something that you yourself want deep inside you. And the new person you just met simply makes those hopes appear. So I would try to explore and enjoy them -- without forgetting that these are dreams and that life will always be different.
