title: "You are out"
---

'You are out'. It rings in my ears. I look around. My face is stone. No one dares to cross my eyes. Some apathic, some looking down, some busy with their phones.

You are out. I turn around, show them my back. Step into the floor.

You are out. Through the corridor, into the elevator, passing by the busy secretaries.

You are out. Out of the building, in the parking lot. 

You are out. In the car, on the road.

I am in. Home. Safety. Comfort.

I cry.
