---
title: Don't search, query!
date: 2023-08-07
---

Every time you are searching for something, you are actually querying it. You try to describe what you are looking for (build a query), sometimes narrow it down (`ext:pdf` or `site:wikipedia.org`) and then execute it in the context that is applicable (your browser, your local file explorer, ...). 

So, time for a change in perspective: Querying is simpler than you think! You have been doing all the time :)
