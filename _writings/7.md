Titles are complicated. _To title or not to title, that is the question._ 

Option A: No titles, just timestamps
Option B: Numbers, 0..n
Option C: Titles

Option A doesn't promote information and makes for ugly URLs (xyz.com/2023080612472013982.html). Option B solves that, but still doesn't convey a lot of information. And sometimes the title is important. Especially in a Zettelkasten-setup, where I will link between files (TODO). Option C solves that, but it is unwieldy again -- a thought doesn't need a title.

I could of course just use separate templates, but I want to dump everything together without worying. What to do?

I guess I'll just do no-title by default (numbering, possibly with timestamp in the frontmatter for archival reasons) and then add a title if needed? But how to display them then in the front-page of this website? An activity grid for nubmered thoughts and a list for actual long-form posts? And in the activity grid I could make high-text posts bold?
