title: Illusions, delusions and dreams.
---

> I can't imagine a future with you anymore.

I never understood that sentence. I had read it quite often --it is a common trope--, but I never understood it intellectually. Until a friend told me about his breakup. And suddenly it made sense -- we all know it. 

Meeting someone, becoming interested... and suddenly your mind goes into overdrive, imagines the most creative scenarios. Going on holidays together, living together, having a house, children, trees and garden and growing old.

And sometimes it just comes crashing down. Reality confronts the dream, and reality wins. Always. Your interest (maybe even partner by then) is different than your dreams. And you can just not imagine it anymore.
