# Happy life in Napoli

I sit on the balcony, a cup of coffee in front of me. A cat lazes on the sill opposite of me, yawning in the sun. Checkered pigeons are hiding in the cracked walls, mysteriously staring at her. The sun is rising and it is hot.

Bright gowns and trousers hang between the streets, slowly rustling in the breeze. An old grandma smiles at me. I smile back, raise my coffee and enjoy the sun. 

People are bustling in the streets far down below, driving their scooters and small cars. Even their honking and shouting sounds cute from here. Nice, relaxing.

The cat moves into a more sunny spot. The pigeons start flying. And I empty my cup. Time to go. Walk around, enjoy the day. I am simply happy today.
