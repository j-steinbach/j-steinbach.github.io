Welcome, dear stranger. This is my site. A collection of random notes and thoughts, in pure text. No images, no ads, just messy writings. 

You will find happy thoughts, sad thoughts, random thoughts and an assortement of peculiar things, notes and odd scribbles. And pretentiousness, because I can and am good at it.

I am doing this for myself, as I want to share and need to share whatever is going on in my head. Too many thoughts, running wildly along the landscape of my mind.

I plan to write at least one note daily, maybe more, never less -- unless I am sick, traveling or dead.

I can not guarantee for the best reading experience, and I can not guarantee that these notes will always be clean and free of violence, drugs, sex, and rock'n'roll. These are my notes, my thoughts, a landscape of my mind.

To cut the pretentiousness, I'll end here. Feel free to browse, explore and even contact me (zilch@mailbox.org). The notes have no order, so either start at the first one, the last one or pick a random number.

--

Some of the notes will be in German, as that is my native tongue. I am a big proponent of writing in your native voice, as there is so much beauty lost in translation. Who knows, maybe I inspire you to learn German? It is in my opinion the most beautiful and precise language for writing. 

But I'll try to translate, with the help of tools, or directly write in English. Like here :)
