Good evening. I'm sitting in the Krook and don't know what to write about. Somethin funny? Something sad? Right now life is good, but I see the points of failure. Relationships. Always complicated. Can't my brain just stop working for some time? Can't I just go and keep calm and collected, without worries? There is no sense or pattern.

Whom am I writing this too? Myself? Her? Definitely not her. I would like to, love to confess, but time is not ready. And her answer might not make me happy. Fear, my old friend. Sadness, failure. Anger, frustration. Shadows of the past.

I want to shout from the highest buildings, scream at the top of my lungs. That I am lost, that I lost my heart, my mind, sanity, clarity, everything. 

But I can't. It won't change anything. It won't magically fix things. It will just make me more confused, vulnerable, sad and depressed.

Time heals, time moves, time flows by. I need to wait, settle down, rest. Do my routine, then meet again. 

There are troubles, woes, fears inside of me. How to deal with them? How to let them out? Anger, shouting, the feeling of being alone. It hurts, it pains, it burns. Oh heart, stop beating. 

Just a call, a conversation. Sweet whispers for a few minutes, with the illusion of a happy end. Cruel world, I know it won't be easy.

Or will it? Will I finally find my happiness? Have things go my way? Find what I've been looking for so long? Yearning, expecting, being hesitant about.

Just a call to see clear. To fly high or fall down, Icarus too close. Yes, she is a sun and I am yearning. Flying, flattering my wings. Wanting to be close, knowing I will burn. I am already burning. It burns in my heart, chest, veins and core. 

Fire flows, and I am light ablaze. 

Just a call, to see clear. I know what I want. I don't know her. Where to get help? Where to find remedy? Bustling life around me, but I am quiet, clear, resting, still. Dead heart, why don't you beat? Badum, badum. Lonely in the night.

Just a call, to see clear. What does she want? Change, growth, companionship. I give her everything and even more, but will she want it? My heart is lost, troubled, beating too fast. Just a call. Will she invite me?

I want, want, want, want it so much. I won't won't won't won't get it so much. That is life, that is the cruel truth. I can't change that.

But what if? What if she wants it too? What if she is also burning? Can we burn together? Will we burn together? Will we burn out?

I don't know, know, know, nothing. Emptiness settles in my mind once again. The heart stoped, the time stopped, the world stopped. What if. What if! Don't give me hope!

I have been here once, got hurt, fell down, got crushed. I don't want to be here again. I want to hope, be bright, be happy.

Why can't it be easy? Long distance, unclear past. I know nothing.

Have I done enough? Have I been clear? Did she get it? My heart is lost, beating outside my chest. It sits on the table, bleeding and burning. I can grasp it, give it to her. Another gift she will not care about.

Why do I feel that way? Why do I feel nothing matters? That I will just end up in hurt? Pain, despair? Why can't it be that she also feels the same?

Just a call and I'll know. If she invites me I'll go. If she doesn't, I'll let her go. 

What if, what if, what if. So many burning questions. It burns, it hurts, my flesh is sizzling. Lay it all out, bare, for the world to see. How I want to call her name, call her, confess to her, cling to her, chase after her.

Why can't I? Why shouldn't I? Just a call, a visit and a confession. And life will get complicated.

I lose my edge, my words, my spirit. Am afraid of past shadows, don't dare to venture into the light. Quietly sitting in the shadows, hiding, feeling safe. Have my small heart beat in smallness. Feel life drift by.

How is it different? How is she different? I changed, want to change. She wants to change. Is not completely broken, like the last one. Stands in life, is troubled by it. I am so afraid. How did we meet? Why do we talk? Daily whispers, the devil's smile.

He always smiles. Stares at me in darkness, shows me the light. And step by step I follow, turn my head, move away. Into the light, into the pain, hurt and madness.

I am crazy, a fool, a lost soul. The devil knows my number, and he calls it every time.

And I answer, answer every time. It feels so good.
